My final project is an educational app that is designed for kids to leanr numbers and solve basic mathmatic equations.
This app can draw numbers one through ten with a built in tutorial.
There are also ten questions for subtraction and ten questions for addition that can check if the answer is incorrect or correct.
To use this app you can change the color and draw for numbers one through ten
For the addition and subtraction questions you can answer the questions and see if the answer is correct or incorrect
